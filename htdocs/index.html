About the Creator

Reuben Ryan Anoran is a 9th-grade student currently facing the pandemic when it comes to school, being unable to go out. He used to spend most of his time in school at the library alone or at the field with his friends and classmates. Now, he currently spends most of his “free” time on Messenger chatting with his friends and watching YouTube videos.

About the Project

Class Clash was made to help other students with coping and adjusting to our current setup of Remote-Learning, made by a student as well. The website-title is based on how the setup feels for the Author; like a war. Some of the content is personally made by the author, while some content is taken from mental-health research (sources can be found in the section with the same name).
